Transparency notice on the use of the AI Tutor

You are using an AI Tutor that automatically generates responses using artificial intelligence. You are not communicating with a human being but with an AI system. The AI Tutor is provided by your education provider via the platform of Alphabees UG (haftungsbeschränkt) and may be linked to knowledge sources, settings and AI providers selected by the education provider.

The AI Tutor's responses are generated automatically based on your inputs, the education provider's configuration and the connected knowledge sources. No human review or approval of each individual response takes place before it is delivered, unless expressly stated otherwise in the respective learning offering. Despite careful technical design, responses may therefore be incomplete, outdated, misleading or incorrect.

The AI Tutor is intended to support learning and to help you navigate the provided learning content. It does not replace any binding information, assessment, examination decision, individual pedagogical decision or any other decision made by your education provider. The official learning materials, examination regulations, instructions and decisions of your education provider remain authoritative.

Please do not enter any confidential information, special categories of personal data or content that is not required for the use of the learning offering. Information on the processing of personal data can be found in the privacy notices of the respective education provider and, where applicable, in the supplementary information on the platform of Alphabees UG (haftungsbeschränkt).

If a response appears to be incorrect, problematic or incomplete, please do not rely on it without verification. In such cases, please contact the designated contact point of your education provider named in the learning offering or use the intended reporting channel so that the response, the underlying knowledge sources or the configuration can be reviewed.

This notice is provided in particular so that you can clearly recognise that you are interacting with an AI system. This information is displayed to you no later than at the time of your first interaction with the AI Tutor and is intended to be transparent, comprehensible and easily accessible.
